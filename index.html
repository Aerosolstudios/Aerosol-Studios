Welcome

Aerosol Studios is a creative project to explore early-stage consumer companies in an open dialogue. It is written by Ewan Mitchell and friends. Ewan is an investor specializing in pre-seed investments in consumer products companies.

He spent 7 years at Barclays, where he analyzed the largest consumer staples companies in the EU and was part of the number 1 ranked team. In 2021, he set up his own consumer products-focused venture capital firm. He went on to raise a fund with a marquee investment in Puresport at a very early stage. He has co-authored award-winning research on the global food system for financial institutions.
